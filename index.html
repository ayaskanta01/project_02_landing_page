Ash Ketchum

He is a 10-year-old Pokémon Trainer from Pallet Town in the Kanto region who has always dreamed of becoming the world's greatest Pokémon Master.

He travels with his pokémon buddy pikachu to different regions of the world to find new pokemons and fight in different tournament to become a better pokémon trainer.
